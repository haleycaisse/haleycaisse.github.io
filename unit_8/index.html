About Me

I am from a small town located in Central Massachusetts. I graduated from Westfield State University with a B.A. in Fine Arts with a concentration in Graphic Design. From there I was a Graphic Designer for a beer distributor on the south shore focusing on print production. More recently, I accepted a job as a Decorative Artist and Freelance Graphic Designer. I am passionate, personable and am driving to be the best designer I can be.
